About Tekken 7

Tekken 7 is a dynamic 3D fighting game that brings the legendary *Tekken* series to new heights. Developed by Bandai Namco, it features a diverse roster of iconic characters like Kazuya, Jin, and Heihachi Mishima. The game introduces the powerful Rage system, with Rage Arts and Rage Drives that deliver epic attacks. The story culminates in the intense, final chapter of the Mishima family feud. With its fast-paced, strategic combat and stunning visuals, *Tekken 7* offers an action-packed experience for both newcomers and seasoned players alike.
